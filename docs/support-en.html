Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Your data is protected using secure infrastructure and encrypted communication methods. Your passwords are not visible to the application developer; authentication is provided through secure infrastructure.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

Yes, you can log in with the same account on different devices. Your data is automatically synchronized.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored on secure infrastructure for the purpose of providing application services. When you delete your account, all your data is permanently deleted.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from Settings > Delete My Account menu. A 7-day waiting period applies after the deletion request. You can cancel the process by logging in during this period. After 7 days, your account and all data are permanently deleted.

Is the app free?

The app is currently free. Paid features may be added in the future.
