At Business Tracker, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how personal data collected through our mobile application is processed, protected, and users' rights.

1. Data Collected

1.1 Identity and Account Information

  • Email address
  • Authentication information (passwords are not visible to or stored by the application developer; they are processed through secure authentication infrastructure)

1.2 User-Entered Content

The following information is entered entirely at user's discretion and manually:

  • Financial records (bank accounts, cash, income-expense records)
  • Client account information (customer and supplier information)
  • Staff information
  • Descriptions, notes, and similar content

1.3 Optional Information

Tax number and similar fields are not mandatory and are entered optionally by users.

2. Data Usage Purposes

Collected data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Providing application functions
  • Account and session management
  • Creating financial records and reports
  • Resolving technical issues
  • Fulfilling legal obligations

Data is not used for advertising, marketing, or user tracking purposes.

3. Data Relationship with User (Apple App Store Perspective)

  • Identity and account information is associated with user account.
  • Financial records, client, and staff information entered by users belong to content created within user's account, not to users themselves.
  • This data is accessible only by the relevant user.

4. Data Security

The following measures are taken for personal data security:

  • SSL/TLS encrypted data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Authorization and access control mechanisms
  • Technical measures to prevent unauthorized access

5. Third-Party Service Providers

The application may utilize the following service providers:

  • Authentication services (Apple ID, Google Account)
  • Database and authentication infrastructure (Supabase)
  • Cloud hosting services

These service providers can access data only for the purpose of fulfilling application functions and to a limited extent.

All third-party service providers are obligated to provide protection equal to or higher than the level specified in this Privacy Policy for user data.

6. Data Retention Period and Account Deletion

  • Data is retained as long as user account is active.
  • Users can delete their account from within the application.
  • A 7-day waiting period applies after account deletion request.
  • After the waiting period, account and all associated data are automatically and permanently deleted.
  • For users signing in with Apple ID, Apple authentication tokens are also revoked during account deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

The application is not intended for children under 13. Personal data from children is not knowingly collected.

8. User Rights

Users have the following rights under applicable regulations:

  • Right to access their data
  • Right to request correction
  • Right to request deletion
  • Right to object to processing
