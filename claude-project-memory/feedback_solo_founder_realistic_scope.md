User is a solo founder/developer on defterappv2 (Turkish SMB bookkeeping app). For any marketing, growth, or business strategy work, default to plans that a single person can execute in a few hours per week with $0-100/month budget.

**Why:** When asked for a marketing campaign plan, I produced a multi-channel always-on engine with freelance editors, paid acquisition, and weekly cadence. User responded: "bu kadar zaman ve para harcayamam ben bu uygulamaya." (I can't spend this much time and money on this app.)

**How to apply:**
- For marketing/growth/business asks: assume solo execution, minimal budget, max 2-4 hours/week of marketing time
- Don't recommend freelancers, paid ad budgets >$100/mo, or content cadences requiring full-time work unless user explicitly asks for ambitious scale
- When generic frameworks (like the campaign-plan skill) produce big plans, immediately offer a "minimum viable" version sized for one person
- Lead with the 2-3 things that will move the needle, not the comprehensive list
- Prefer one-time builds (ASO, referral, evergreen blog post) over recurring cadences
